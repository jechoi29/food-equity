How to Implement "Recipe Radar" In Your Food Bank

Malnutrition is a prevalent issue within the US; many families that are affected by poverty and hunger often to not have access to fresh, affordable ingredients. On top of that, dietary restrictions may hinder them further in obtaining the nutrients they need to survive. Have your local food bank implement "Recipe Radar" to help foster a healthier, happier community.

  1. Open Recipe Radar on a computer at your local food bank
  2. Allow food bank guests to input their dietary restrictions and select a recipe of their choice
  3. Print out the recipe for the guests to use as a shopping list in your bank
